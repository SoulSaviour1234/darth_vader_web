Coffee Lovers' Paradise

Ingredients required to make coffee

  • Ground coffee
  • Hot water
  • Coffee maker (e.g., French press, drip machine, moka pot, etc.)
  • Mug or cup
  • Spoon or stirrer
  • Optional: Sugar, milk, cream, or sweetener
  • Kettle or pot (to boil water)
  • Coffee filter (if using drip method)

Steps to make coffee

  1. Boil water.
  2. Add ground coffee to French press.
  3. Pour hot water over the coffee.
  4. Stir gently.
  5. Place lid and let it steep for 4 minutes.
  6. Press the plunger down slowly.
  7. Pour into a mug and enjoy.

Types of Coffee

Americano
Espresso diluted with hot water, giving it a similar strength to regular brewed coffee but with a different flavor.
Cappuccino
Equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. Has a stronger espresso flavor compared to a latte.
Latte
A shot of espresso mixed with a larger amount of steamed milk and a light layer of foam on top. Smooth and creamy.
Iced Coffee
Regular brewed coffee served cold, typically poured over ice and optionally sweetened or mixed with milk.
